## Getting Started

### Sign Up

1. Open the Tastizo Delivery app.
2. Tap **Sign Up**.
3. **Step 1** – Basic details (name, phone, email, vehicle).
4. **Step 2** – Document verification:
   - Profile photo
   - Aadhar card
   - PAN card
   - Driving license
5. Use **camera** to capture documents.
6. Submit for verification.

### Sign In

1. Enter phone number.
2. Verify OTP.
3. Complete sign-in.

## Going Online

### Go Online

1. Go to **Feed** (home).
2. Tap **Go Online**.
3. Ensure you are in a **service zone**.
4. You will receive order notifications.

### Go Offline

1. Tap **Go Offline**.
2. You will stop receiving new orders.

### Map View

- **Hotspot** – View zones and demand.
- **Select drop** – Drop location selection.
- **Swipe** – Switch between map and home sections.

## Accepting Orders

### New Order Notification

1. **Sound** – Alert for new orders.
2. **Order card** – Order details appear.
3. Tap **Accept** to take the order.

### Order Details

- Restaurant name and address
- Customer address
- Items
- Payment type (cash/online)
- Estimated earnings

### Decline

- Tap **Decline** if you cannot accept.

## Completing a Delivery

### Order Flow

1. **Accepted** – Order assigned to you.
2. **Reached pickup** – At restaurant.
3. **Confirm order ID** – Verify order ID.
4. **Reached drop** – At customer location.
5. **Complete delivery** – Finish delivery.

### Step-by-Step

1. **Navigate to restaurant** – Use map.
2. Tap **Reached pickup** – When you arrive.
3. **Confirm order ID** – Match order ID with restaurant.
4. **Navigate to customer** – Use map.
5. Tap **Reached drop** – When you arrive.
6. **Collect payment** – If cash on delivery.
7. Tap **Complete delivery** – Finish.

### Bill Image (Cash Orders)

1. For cash orders, tap **Capture bill**.
2. Use **camera** to capture payment receipt.
3. Upload to complete.

### Chat

- **Chat with customer** – For delivery questions.
- **Chat with restaurant** – For pickup questions.

## Earnings & Wallet

### Pocket

1. Go to **Pocket**.
2. View:
   - **Earnings** – Today’s and total earnings.
   - **Wallet balance** – Available balance.
   - **Requests** – Withdrawal requests.

### Earnings

1. Go to **Earnings**.
2. View:
   - Daily earnings
   - Per-trip earnings
   - Period earnings

### Withdraw

1. Go to **Pocket** or **Wallet**.
2. Tap **Withdraw**.
3. Enter amount.
4. Submit request.

### Cash Limit

- **Cash collected** – Cash collected from customers.
- **Deposit** – Deposit cash to wallet.
- **Limit** – Follow cash limits.
