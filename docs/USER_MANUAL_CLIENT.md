## Getting Started

### Sign Up / Sign In

1. Open the Tastizo app.
2. Set your **delivery location** (top bar).
3. Choose **Sign In** or **Sign Up**.
4. You can sign in with:
   - **Phone** – OTP via SMS
   - **Email** – OTP via email
   - **Google** – Google account

### Set Delivery Location

1. Tap the **location bar** at the top.
2. Allow location access or enter an address manually.
3. Confirm your delivery area.

## Placing an Order

### Browse Restaurants

1. Go to **Delivery** (home).
2. Browse restaurants by:
   - **Search** – Restaurant or dish name
   - **Categories** – Cuisine type
   - **Collections** – Featured lists
   - **Offers** – Discounts and deals

### Open a Restaurant

1. Tap a restaurant card.
2. View menu, ratings, delivery time, and offers.

### Add Items to Cart

1. Tap the **+** button on a dish.
2. Choose **variants** (size, add-ons) if available.
3. Add **quantity** if needed.
4. Tap **Add to Cart**.

---

## Cart & Checkout

### View Cart

1. Tap the **cart icon** in the top bar.
2. Or tap the **cart bar** at the bottom.

### Edit Cart

- **Change quantity** – Use +/- buttons.
- **Remove item** – Tap the trash icon.
- **Apply coupon** – Enter code in the coupon field.

### Checkout

1. Tap **Proceed to checkout**.
2. **Delivery address** – Select or add new.
3. **Payment method** – Razorpay, Cash, or Wallet.
4. **Order note** – Optional instructions.
5. **Cutlery** – Toggle if needed.
6. Tap **Place order**.

### Payment

- **Online** – Payment via Razorpay.
- **Cash** – Pay on delivery.
- **Wallet** – Use Tastizo wallet balance.

## Orders

### View Orders

1. Go to **Profile** → **My Orders**.
2. Or tap the **cart bar** and open **Orders**.

### Order Status

- **Placed** – Order received
- **Confirmed** – Restaurant has accepted
- **Preparing** – Being prepared
- **Ready** – Ready for pickup
- **Out for delivery** – On the way
- **Delivered** – Delivered

### Track Order

1. Open the active order.
2. View live map and status.
3. **Chat** – Contact restaurant or delivery partner.

### Reorder

1. Open a past order.
2. Tap **Reorder** to add items to cart.

## Profile & Account

### Profile

1. Go to **Profile**.
2. Edit profile:
   - Tap **Edit profile**.
   - Change profile photo (camera or gallery).
   - Update name, mobile, email, date of birth, gender.

### Addresses

1. Go to **Profile** → **Addresses**.
2. **Add** – New delivery address.
3. **Edit** – Update existing address.
4. **Delete** – Remove address.
5. Set **default** address.

### Favorites

1. Go to **Profile** → **Favorites**.
2. View saved restaurants.
3. Tap a restaurant to open.

### Wallet

1. Go to **Profile** → **Wallet**.
2. View balance.
3. Add money (if applicable).

### Settings

1. Go to **Profile** → **Settings**.
2. **Veg mode** – Show only vegetarian options.
3. **Notifications** – Order and offer alerts.
4. **Logout** – Sign out.

## Dining

1. Go to **Dining**.
2. Browse dine-in restaurants.
3. Tap a restaurant to see details.
4. **Book a table** – Choose date, time, guests.
5. **View offers** – Dining-specific deals.
